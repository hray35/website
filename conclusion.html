Conclusion

You may not have ever heard of light fidelity or Li-Fi. It may sound futuristic, but the foundation for the technology already exists. The world should accept this technology because it is more secure than wireless fidelity (Wi-Fi) and 100 times faster. The security alone makes it perfect for government and vulnerable businesses who need to worry about things like Ransomware. If the technology is promoted and advertised more, more investors and government agencies will research and develop this technology. Gartner is not always correct, and they indeed have this one wrong. Li-Fi is the technology that will replace wireless.
